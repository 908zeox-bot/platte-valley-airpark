FROM THE FLIGHT DECK

Cleared for Commercial

Conversational study sessions for pilots preparing for the FAA Commercial Pilot checkride.

Each episode is a dialogue between Alex, a student pilot, and Jordan, an experienced CFI — covering SR22T systems, commercial maneuvers, regulations, weather, and emergency procedures. Built for technically advanced aircraft. No filler, just the knowledge you need.

Produced by Altitude Z Aviation | Hosted at Platte Valley Airpark (18V)

Episodes

EP 525:32

Emergency Procedures and Human Factors

Engine failures, electrical emergencies, CAPS decisions, IMSAFE, DECIDE model, hypoxia, spatial disorientation, and a rapid-fire V-speeds and limitations close.

EP 426:16

Weather and Aerodynamics

Pressure systems, fronts, icing, thunderstorms, lift, drag, load factor, stall speed in turns — thinking like an engineer, not just a pilot.

EP 325:08

Regulations Oral Exam Prep

Full mock oral exam — Jordan as the DPE, Alex in the hot seat. Certificate requirements, endorsements, ARROW documents, airworthiness directives, maintenance, and flight operations rules.

EP 225:20

Commercial Maneuvers Mastery

Chandelles, lazy eights, steep turns, eights on pylons, power-off 180s — the mechanics, the standards, and how to fly them in the SR22T.

EP 125:34

SR22T Systems Deep Dive

Cirrus SR22T systems from the ground up — engine, fuel, avionics, pressurization, CAPS, and the questions every DPE will ask.
