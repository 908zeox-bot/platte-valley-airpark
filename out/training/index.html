Pilot Resources

A few things we've found useful in our own training. Take what helps, ignore what doesn't.

Cleared for Commercial

Conversational checkride prep — a student and CFI working through SR22T systems, maneuvers, regs, weather, and emergencies. Produced by Altitude Z Aviation.

AI-assisted content. Use as a study aid alongside official FAA materials and your CFI.

EP 1

SR22T Systems

Engine, fuel, avionics, pressurization, and CAPS.

EP 2

Commercial Maneuvers

Chandelles, lazy eights, steep turns, eights on pylons, power-off 180s.

EP 3

Regulations

Certificate requirements, ARROW documents, airworthiness directives, and flight rules.

EP 4

Weather and Aerodynamics

Pressure systems, fronts, icing, lift, drag, and load factor.

EP 5

Emergency Procedures

Engine failures, CAPS decisions, IMSAFE, DECIDE model, and human factors.
