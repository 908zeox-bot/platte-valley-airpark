For over 60 years, the Front Range's backyard runway.

Nestled in the heart of Colorado's Front Range, Platte Valley Airpark (18V) isn't just an airport; it's a vibrant community hub built on a shared passion for aviation. For over six decades, pilots, families, and enthusiasts have gathered here, drawn by the welcoming atmosphere, excellent facilities, and breathtaking views of the Rockies.

Our commitment to general aviation is reflected in every detail, from our meticulously maintained runways to our active calendar of community events. We believe flying should be accessible, enjoyable, and a source of lasting friendships.

"Whether you're flying in for the first time or you've been based here for decades — you're family."
